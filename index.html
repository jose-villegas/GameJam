The Idea - The Theme (What Do We Do Now)

We created the game around a simple gameplay idea, it is about kids saving adults from the shock of the society because monsters like zombies, giant angry rabipelados and bunny cyclops ghost are haunting the city of CTM, adults don't know what to do now but the kids do.

The Plot

In the year 2015, adults are submerged in their own world and concerns. suddenly, weird things begin to happen behind their backs...

ZOMBIES start to attack!, bunny cyclops ghosts emerge from the underworld! and mutant rabipelados start eating people!

WHAT DO WE DO NOW? WHO WILL HELP US? WHAT TO DO?

Adults are shocked, and in a panic, they don’t know how to react! they were so busy and stressed, they can’t handle this situation by themselves!

But luckily, the kids are here to help! For some reason they are the ones able to solve this problem and destroy the enemies, to help everyone and bring them to safety! So, go! choose your favorite character, and start this weird adventure to save everyone!
